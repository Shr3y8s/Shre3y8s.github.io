Get In Shape For Dummies: 4-Step Guide

This step-by-step strategy guide will tell you everything you need to get healthy starting today.

Step 1

WHY DO YOU WANT TO GET IN SHAPE?

Think about what inspires you to make a change..

This step is important for making your goals clear.

Also helps you to stay motivated.

Step 2

HOW TO EAT / NUTRITION

Most of the success to getting in shape is understanding and implementing nutrition.

This doesnt have to be complicated or life changing..

Food should be focused towards your goals.

For example if you want to build muscle, it is wise to have more protein throughout the day.

The trick is to think about foods you enjoy and find ways to make those options healthy and still taste good.

Step 3

EXERCISE

Implementing some type of exercise everyday will help you get to your goal.

Start by identifying an exercise routine specific to your goal.

Start with easy movements and don't be afraid to ask for help if your unsure about where to start.

Step 4

CONSISTENCY

The most IMPORTANT step to getting in shape is CONSISTENCY.

Consistently making healthy choices everyday is what will ultimatly make or break your ability to achieve your goals.

If you don't take the time to do the neccessary things everyday... you cannot expect to succeed.

Are YOU willing to put in the time?
